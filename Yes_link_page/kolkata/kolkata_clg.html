About Department - BCA
As the IT industry continues to expand rapidly, the demand for computer professionals is increasing day by day. This expansion has created numerous opportunities for computer graduates. The Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA), a 4-year Honours degree course, is one of the most popular choices among students aiming to build a career in the IT field.
Techno India University, Kolkata, a prestigious institution under the Techno India Group, offers the BCA Honours program, designed to prepare students for both industry and higher academic pursuits.This course is affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT, formerly known as WBUT).
Eligibility:
As per the guidelines of Techno India University, candidates who have passed Class XII (Science/Commerce/Arts) are eligible for admission to the BCA program, provided they had any one of the following subjects: Mathematics, Business Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Computer Application, or Information Practices.
Duration of the Course:
The BCA Course is a 4-year Honours degree, divided into 8 semesters.
Course Fee:
The BCA course fee is inclusive of training programs, seminars, workshops, and other associated fees such as those for fresher's welcome, farewell events, and tech fests.
Faculties and Staff:
The department is equipped with a team of highly experienced professors and industry-trained faculty members who provide academic and practical support to students.
Future Scope:
  • Scope for Job and Entrepreneurship:

Techno India University arranges multiple campus recruitment drives, enabling students to secure placements in top IT companies. Being a 4-year Honours degree, BCA graduates are also eligible for government jobs and teaching positions.

Students are also encouraged to pursue entrepreneurship. The department conducts training programs on the latest technologies to help students become self-reliant. The university houses a dedicated Entrepreneurship Cell that supports and nurtures students' startup ideas.

  • Scope of Higher Studies:

After completing BCA, students can pursue Master of Computer Applications (MCA) a 2-year postgraduate course equivalent to B.Tech.

They are also eligible for other postgraduate programs like MBA or M.Sc. in IT, depending on their interest and career goals..
